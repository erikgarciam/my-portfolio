About Me

I was born in Paramount, California. Around five years later we moved to the High Desert and the city of Hesperia, California. I lived there all the way though highschool. I still refer Hesperia as home base since most of my family and friends are located there.

In High School I had enrolled in a Class called IT Essentials where I learned that technology was my passion. It is through IT Essentials that I enrolled into Cisco's Networking Academy. It was through these joint opportunities that I learned more about computers and networking. These skills by themselves weren't enough for me though because I also wanted to learn how computers and software work. When applying to the university I already knew I wanted a mix of hardware and software mixed in so I applied for a major in Computer Engineering.

I then embarked on my university journey and am now a rising junior, which means I will be starting my third year at California State University, Fullerton.
